Melody's Clay County Hunt

📍 Clay County, Missouri

Half-day guided hunt along the Missouri River bluffs in Clay County. We cover south-facing draws, creek-edge timber, and wood lines bordering farm fields — the classic NW Missouri habitat. Great for beginners and experienced hunters alike.

Half day · 4 hrs Up to 6 guests All skill levels Peak: mid April

Bluff & Timber Full Day

📍 Clay County, Missouri

Full-day immersive hunt covering multiple habitat types — river bluff faces, south-slope timber, old field edges, and creek drainages. Includes habitat education so you leave knowing exactly what to look for on your own.

Full day · 8 hrs Up to 4 guests Lunch included Peak: April 15–25
